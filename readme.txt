Fully featured access to content-distribution network named Article Exchange.


== Description ==

Wordpress Article Exchange is the perfect solution for any wordpress website which needs to sync data with Article Exchange CDN.

* Syndication of content
* Push your own content to CDN AE  (with or without images)
* Remove your own content from Article Exchange
* Find and import content to your blog (with or without images)
* Field-mapping to improve use-ability
